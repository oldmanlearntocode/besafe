NOTES ON CHARTS AND GRAPHS

Some notes about the charts and graphs on this website:


  • The charts are essentially a reflection of deceased LGBTI+ individuals that were victims of crime in general or alleged victims of hate crimes as reported in the media over time.
  • We counted victims not source reports or incidents.
  • It is highly likely that there are many more incidents and victims of hate crimes but these went unreported or undetected.
  • It is possible that our search engine did not pick up all of the incidents so feel free to guide us in the right direction.
  • Total number of victims: Number of victims corresponding with the wall of remembrance and source reports. There might be several source reports focusing on the death of one person.
  • Province of Incident: Province where the incident took place as reported in the media. We counted victims per province.
  • Year of Incident: Year that the incident took place as reported in the media. We counted victims per year.
  • Age of Victim: Age of the victim as reported in Media. We counted victims per age group.
